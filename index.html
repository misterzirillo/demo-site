About us

How it works

Forming a Montana LLC to purchase, hold and sell any personal property that has a title is fast, easy and legal. You simply provide us the desired name of the LLC, the names of the members and we handle the rest for you. You and your members do not need to reside in Montana, your LLC is the legal resident of the state. Your LLC may purchase, or transfer the ownership, of any motor vehicle (or any other personal property with a title) to your newly formed LLC. You may operate that vehicle in any state if you comply with the laws of the state where the vehicle is located.

Plan

Pick the name for your new Montana LLC, and decide who the members will be. Provide that information to us and we handle the rest.

Create

Once we receive your desired name and the names of the members we will check to determine if the name is available and file all necessary paperwork with the Montana Secretary of State utilizing our address as your LLC’s principle address and listing us as the registered agent. We will forward your LLC paperwork to you as soon as we receive it from the Secretary of State.

Operate

With your newly formed Montana LLC you can purchase a new vehicle or transfer ownership of an existing vehicle to the new LLC thus avoiding potential sales taxes and costly registration fees. We will handle the registration of your vehicle with the Montana DMV and forward your registration and plates to you.

Manage

We can handle all subsequent yearly filings along with the registration renewals for you and we can forward any mail received on your LLC’s behalf. In addition, we can assist in winding down your LLC and help filing any necessary paperwork to potentially avoid local use taxes. All you need to do is enjoy your new vehicle.
